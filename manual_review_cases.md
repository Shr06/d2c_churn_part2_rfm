# Manual Review Cases

The following customers were selected for manual review because their retention decision is not straightforward. While the automated segmentation model assigns each customer to a segment, these customers exhibit mixed behavioural signals that require additional business judgement.

**Assigned Segment:** Loyal Customers

### Metrics

* Recency: 51 days
* Frequency: 4 orders
* Monetary Value: ₹2,989.58
* Return Rate: 25%
* Support Tickets: 2
* Sessions: 2

### Why Review is Needed

The customer demonstrates strong purchase behaviour and high spending, which supports the Loyal Customers classification. However, the presence of multiple support tickets and a relatively high return rate may indicate emerging dissatisfaction.

### Reasoning

The customer remains highly valuable, but dissatisfaction signals should be addressed before they affect future purchasing behaviour.
